THE PASALUBONG CENTER

The perfect place to find locally made ylang-ylang products, as well as souvenirs and handmade goods, at reasonable prices is the Anao Pasalubong Center, which is situated in the municipality of Anao. Our entire product line is made by MSMEs and cooperatives which are established in Anao, Tarlac. Our ylang-ylang essential oil, under our primary brand Aroma Anao, is extracted from our homegrown ylang-ylang trees and processed in our locality's laboratory and extraction rooms. We aim, not only to support local businesses by serving as a means to sell their quality ylang-ylang products, but also to promote the ylang-ylang industry of the municipality to a wider audience.

MUNICIPALITY OF ANAO

Anao is the smallest municipality of Tarlac. It is located in the northeastern part of Tarlac with eighteen barangays. While stories behind its name vary, many locals believe that the name Anao came from anahaw—the Philippines' national leaf—because of its abundance in the locality in the past. Currently, it is occupied by hectares of agricultural land with its streets filled with ylang-ylang trees.


The municipality aims to become a sustainable community that is committed to promote the general welfare of the locality. It also envisions itself as Bagong Anao that leads the province's agriculture and business.

YLANG-YLANG CAPITAL

Despite being the smallest municipality of Tarlac, Anao is known as the province's “Ylang-Ylang Capital” and has produced ylang-ylang essential oil of export-graded quality. Its ylang-ylang industry, which started in the 1990s, is a means of primary livelihood for the people in addition to farming. Locals believe in the potential of this industry as it is currently receiving support from DOST and DTI, and has also received a foreign direct investment from the Korean International Cooperation Agency in 2013. The local government is making efforts to upscale the industry to cater to the increasing demand, not only domestically but also internationally.

OUR YLANG-YLANG PRODUCTION PROCESS

1.  Picking of raw materials
Ylang-ylang flowers are picked from the trees in Anao.
2.  Extraction of ylang-ylang flowers
Picked ylang-ylang flowers undergo quality checking and are extracted at the ylang-ylang extraction room.
3.  Storing and checking of finished goods
Extracted ylang-ylang essential oils are weighed and portioned for production in the ylang-ylang laboratory room. After the production process, they undergo quality and safety checking. Afterwards, they are packed and stored as finished goods.
