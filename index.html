OUR BEST-SELLING

Ylang-Ylang Essential Oil

Our ylang-ylang essential oil is made by our local government's own brand of ylang-ylang products—Aroma Anao. It is extracted from our locally grown ylang-ylang flowers. It is also produced in our own laboratory facility within the locality. Aroma Anao ylang-ylang essential oil is produced in 10 ml glass bottles and is sold individually or in bulk.
